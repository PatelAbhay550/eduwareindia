## Overview {#overview}

- RRB NTPC 2025 (CEN 05/2024) offers a total of **11588** vacancies with **8,113 graduate‑level** and **3,445 undergraduate‑level** posts.  
- **Graduate CBT‑1:**  5 – 24 June 2025  
- **Undergraduate CBT‑1:** 07 August – 08 September 2025  
- Selection process: CBT‑1 → CBT‑2 → Skill Test (CBAT / Typing as applicable).

## Preparation Strategy {#preparation-strategy}

### 1. Six‑Month Roadmap
| Month | Focus |
|-------|-------|
| 1–2 | Build basics in high‑weight Maths topics; start daily current‑affairs notes |
| 3–4 | Intensive Reasoning practice; weekly full‑length mocks |
| 5 | Timed section‑wise drills; refine GA fact sheets |
| 6 | Mock‑test marathon, error logs, rapid revision |

### 2. 90‑Day Sprint
- **Mocks:** 2 full CBTs + 3 sectional tests per week.  
- **Target Accuracy:** Maths ≥ 70 %, Reasoning ≥ 75 %, GA ≥ 65 %.  
- **Revision Cycles:** Every Sunday, revisit weak topics identified in mocks.

### 3. Daily Routine Example
| Slot | Activity | Duration |
|------|----------|---------:|
| 6 am | Current Affairs | 20 min |
| 7 – 8 am | Maths concept + drills | 1 h |
| 5 – 6 pm | Reasoning sets | 1 h |
| 9 pm | GA quiz + PYQ review | 45 min |

## FAQs {#faqs}

**Q1. What subjects are in CBT 1 & 2?**  
Mathematics, General Intelligence & Reasoning, and General Awareness.

**Q2. Is the syllabus identical for both CBTs?**  
Yes. CBT 2 only increases the number of questions.

**Q3. What is a safe score to aim for?**  
Target ≥ 80/100 in CBT 1; ≥ 95/120 in CBT 2 with high accuracy.

**Q4. How should I prepare General Awareness?**  
Daily current‑affair capsules plus systematic static GK revision.

**Q5. Are previous‑year papers necessary?**  
Absolutely—PYQs reveal question patterns and help optimize speed and accuracy.
