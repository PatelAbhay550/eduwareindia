The Union Public Service Commission (UPSC) Civil Services Examination is one of India's most prestigious competitive exams. This comprehensive guide provides a complete roadmap for UPSC preparation.

## Understanding the UPSC Exam Pattern

The UPSC Civil Services Examination consists of three stages:

### Stage 1: Preliminary Examination (Prelims)
- **Paper 1:** General Studies (200 marks)
- **Paper 2:** Civil Services Aptitude Test (CSAT) (200 marks)
- **Nature:** Objective type, multiple choice questions
- **Duration:** 2 hours each

### Stage 2: Main Examination (Mains)
- **9 Papers** covering various subjects
- **Written examination** of descriptive type
- **Total Marks:** 1750
- **Duration:** 3 hours each paper
